About Us

Our Vision

Solving the problems of the people that we had faced ourselves is the main motto of our team. Our goal is to make it easier for the upcoming students to learn to code and excel in their lives. There is no better way to learn other than learning things in your own native language, ODIA. Thus, we aim at providing all the educational facilities at free of cost in our own native language.

Who We Are?

We are a small group of Engineering students who have come forward to educate the upcoming youth of our state, ODISHA, and help them in achieving their dreams by teaching them the required technical skills that the present world demands. We are providing the education facility completely free of cost so that each and every student has access to it.
